Slow Cooker Pulled Pork BBQ

A savory blend of very little that will make a ton of food with remarkable ease
See the recipe

Ingredients

  • Big hunk 'o pork
  • One jar of BBQ sauce
  • One onion
  • 1/2 a cup of chicken broth

Follow these steps very closely.

  1. Buy a hunk of pork, size depedent upon the hunger you plan on experiencing over the next several days and your ability to reconcile the death of a charismatic pig
  2. Put said chunk of pork in your weathered, yet functional crock pot
  3. Add one (or two) jars of BBQ sauce, one diced onion and 1/2 a cup of chicken broth
  4. Cook on low for roughly 8 hours
  5. Remove and savagely shred with two forks and impunity
  6. Remove juices from slow cooker and simmer on med heat until thick. Add to crock pot with shredded animal flesh and more BBQ sauce. Cook for another hour. Weep for innocence lost
